# Customer Shopping Trend_Analysis
Business Problem : The objective of this project is to analyze customer purchasing behavior and identify key factors influencing revenue. The goal is to help businesses understand customer segments, evaluate the effectiveness of discounts, and optimize sales strategies.

# Dataset
Customer transactional dataset (~3.9K records) <br>
Includes product details, customer demographics, purchase behavior, discounts, and payment methods

# Tools & Technologies Used
Python (pandas, NumPy) – Data cleaning & preprocessing <br>
SQL Server – Data storage and analytical queries <br>
Power BI – Dashboarding & visualization <br>

# Project Workflow
1.Data Cleaning & Exploration (Python) <br>
  Handled missing values and standardized columns <br>
  Performed exploratory data analysis to understand distributions and patterns <br>
2.Data Storage & Processing (SQL Server) <br>
  Loaded cleaned dataset into SQL Server <br>
  Performed aggregations and analytical queries to extract insights <br>
3.Visualization (Power BI) <br>
  Built interactive dashboards to track KPIs and customer trends <br>
  Enabled filtering by demographics, product categories, and regions <br>

# Key Insights & Business Conclusions
1.Customer Distribution <br>
  Customer base (~3.9K) is evenly distributed across age groups <br>
  No single demographic segment dominates revenue contribution <br>

2.Subscription Impact <br>
  ~73% of revenue comes from non-subscribed customers <br>
  Average purchase value is similar across both groups (~$59) <br>
Insight: Subscription program currently has limited impact on customer spending behavior. <br>

3.Revenue Drivers <br>
  Clothing and Accessories are top-performing categories <br>
  Free Shipping and Express Shipping contribute highest revenue <br>
  Credit Card and PayPal are dominant payment methods <br>
  Revenue is concentrated in a few high-performing regions <br>
Insight: Customer purchase decisions are influenced more by product category and convenience than loyalty programs. <br>

4.Discount Effectiveness <br>
  Non-discounted purchases generate higher overall revenue <br>
  Discounts do not significantly increase spending <br>
Insight: Current discount strategies may not be effectively driving incremental revenue. <br>

5.Customer Satisfaction <br>
  Average rating: ~3.75 (moderate satisfaction level) <br>
Insight: Improving customer experience could enhance retention and long-term value. <br>

# Business Recommendations
  Redesign subscription model to provide stronger value propositions <br>
  Optimize discount strategies based on actual impact <br>
  Focus on high-performing product categories and regions <br>
  Improve customer experience to increase satisfaction and retention <br>
